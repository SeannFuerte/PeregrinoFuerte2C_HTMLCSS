About Us

God Eater Burst

  Welcome to the thrilling world of God Eater Burst, an exhilarating action RPG that immerses players in a post-apocalyptic universe teeming with deadly creatures and intense battles. Developed by Shift and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment, God Eater Burst is a refined and expanded version of the original God Eater game, offering enhanced gameplay, an enriched storyline, and new features that heighten the gaming experience.

  In God Eater Burst, you step into a world on the brink of annihilation. The Earth, once a thriving planet, has been overrun by monstrous entities known as Aragami. These terrifying creatures are immune to conventional weapons, driving humanity to the edge of extinction. However, hope is not lost. An elite force of warriors, known as the God Eaters, have emerged, armed with advanced biological weapons called God Arcs. These unique weapons, capable of transforming between powerful blades and high-impact guns, are humanity's last line of defense against the Aragami menace.

  The game offers a rich narrative that delves deep into the mysteries of the Aragami and the origins of the God Eaters. As you progress through the story, you'll uncover secrets, forge alliances, and face moral dilemmas that will shape the future of humanity. The enhanced storyline in God Eater Burst provides a deeper, more immersive experience, keeping players engaged and emotionally invested in the fate of the characters and the world.

  One of the standout features of God Eater Burst is its dynamic combat system. The game allows players to switch seamlessly between melee and ranged attacks, enabling strategic gameplay that requires quick thinking and adaptability. Whether you prefer to slash through enemies with a devastating sword or shoot from a distance with a powerful rifle, the choice is yours. The refined gameplay mechanics ensure smooth, responsive controls and a satisfying combat experience.

  Customization plays a significant role in God Eater Burst. Players can personalize their God Arcs, develop unique combat strategies, and tailor their characters' abilities to suit their playstyle. The game offers a vast array of upgrades, skills, and equipment, allowing for endless combinations and a highly personalized gaming experience. This level of customization not only enhances gameplay but also adds depth and replayability.

  Multiplayer mode in God Eater Burst takes the excitement to a whole new level. Join forces with friends or other players online to tackle challenging missions and formidable Aragami. Cooperation and teamwork are crucial as you strategize and combine your strengths to defeat powerful foes. The multiplayer experience fosters a sense of community and camaraderie, making every victory all the more rewarding.

  In addition to its action-packed gameplay and compelling storyline, God Eater Burst features stunning visuals and a captivating soundtrack. The detailed graphics bring the post-apocalyptic world to life, while the music sets the tone for epic battles and emotional moments. Every element of the game has been carefully crafted to provide an immersive and unforgettable experience.

  Join us in the world of God Eater Burst, where every battle is a fight for survival and every decision can change the course of history. Equip your God Arc, step into the shoes of a God Eater, and face the Aragami threat head-on. The fate of humanity lies in your hands. Are you ready to rise to the challenge and become a legend?
